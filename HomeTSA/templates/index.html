About Us

As our first time doing software development, three young students from Cypress Ranch High school-Sanmay Sarada, Sharvay Srivastava, Sid Singh- have taken upon this challenge of software development by using AI.

AI software Derm Engine for TSA nationals.

The AI works by preprocessing and toning data from different sources such as hospital and research centers. It then converts the data for the AI to use and teaches the computer to recognize diseases which will help to diagnose the disease for the user.

Student developing the AI::

  • Sharvay Srivastava: AI expert and head programmer.
  • Sanmay Sarada: Secondary AI Coder and head presentation expert
  • Sid Singh: AI programmer as well as presenter
  • Siddharth Radhakrishnan: TSA student/mentor for AI

These four students have worked relentlessly in the past 3 months to provide TSA national judges with the best software possible for these relatively amateur coders. Both the main programmers completed a 35 hours course online to understand the basics as well as many advanced concepts in AI software developing. They also took courses in HTML and CSS to provide a visually appealing platform to run the program
